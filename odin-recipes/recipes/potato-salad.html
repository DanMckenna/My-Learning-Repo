Potato Salad

The perfect summertime side dish!

This dish has the perfect blend of both tangy and salty flavors that will keep your guests asking for more.

Feel free to add/replace any ingredients you like. Make your own personal touch!

Ingredients

  • 6 medium potatoes
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup of mayo
  • 6 hard-boiled eggs peeled and diced
  • 1 small onion finely chopped
  • 1 cup of chopped celery
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Place potatoes in a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 20 minutes. Drain and set aside to cool.
  2. Peel and dice potatoes. Transfer to a large bowl; toss with hard-cooked eggs, onion, celery, and 1 teaspoon salt.
  3. Stir vinegar and mayo gently into the bowl of potato salad until evenly coated.
  4. Season to taste then serve and enjoy!
