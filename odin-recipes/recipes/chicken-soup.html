Chicken Soup

A family classic!

Chciken soup is a staple dish for those cold days when you need something to warm up both your body and spirit.

Follow this simple recipe to make good old fashioned soup just like mom.

Ingredients

  • One 3-pound whole chicken
  • Your choice of vegetables (carrots, celery, onion)
  • Chicken broth or bouillon
  • Season with salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Make the stock by boiling the chicken and veggies until the meat is falling off the bone.
  2. Remove the chicken and cut into pieces (discard skin and bones).
  3. Sprinkle with capers, salt, and pepper; cook for 3 minutes on one side. Turn salmon fillets over; continue to cook until salmon flakes easily with a fork, about 5 minutes.
  4. Strain the vegetables (reserving the stock) and cut into pieces. Rinse the pot.
  5. Return everything to the pot. Heat the soup through and season to taste.
  6. Optional: If you want chicken noodle soup, add cooked noodles to the pot just before serving.
